Squier Vintage Modified Stratocaster – Vintage White

With a body based off of one of the most notable guitars in history and dressed in vintage white, the Squier Vintage Modified Ëœ70s Stratocaster electric guitar looks wonderfully familiar. Squier tweaks the classic to give it a custom look and feel. Modified means adding new twists to familiar designs. Squier's Vintage Modified series excels at just that, imparting hot-output chop-shop sound, feel and value to traditional instrument designs.

Squier Vintage Modified Stratocaster – 3-Tone Sunburst

The Squier Vintage Modified Stratocaster electric guitar is constructed of basswood which has excellent sustain and a great resonance at mid and high frequencies. Three Duncan Designed SC-101 single-coil pickups spruce up sound of the guitar. Squier also included a rosewood fretboard, five-way pickup switch, three-ply pickguard, numbered volume and tone control knobs, vintage-style synchronized tremolo bridge with vintage-style tremolo arm, vintage-style chrome tuners, chrome hardware and an engraved neck plate.

Classic Design Cutaway Dreadnought Acoustic-Electric

The sweet-sounding Classic Design Series CD-60CE Cutaway Dreadnought Acoustic-Electric Guitar features a spruce top, mahogany back and sides, scalloped bracing, 20-fret sonokeling (Indian rosewood) fingerboard, sonokeling bridge with compensated saddle, chrome hardware and die-cast chrome tuners. Features a Fishman Isys III pickup system with active preamp, tuner, and volume and tone controls.

Squier Standard Jazz Bass

The Squier Standard Jazz Bass features many Fender® Jazz Bass appointments, including an offset-waist body and an ultra-slim fast-action neck. Four-saddled hardtail bridge and two Jazz full-range bi-pole pickups are installed on the top. Standard maple neck is topped with a 20-fret rosewood fingerboard.
